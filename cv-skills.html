Me as a worker

I have very strong work ethic and I want to be proud of the work I produce. I'm hard on myself about the quality of my work. I am not one to easily give up on and if I have a vision I do everything I can to make it work. I expect the same of my colleagues as I expect of myself nothing less nothing more.

As a team member

I'm at my best when working in small 4-10 memeber groups as I have a competitive nature. Seeing other members of the team do well makes me want to try even harder and that is how I can push myself to my limits an sometimes even beyond them. I am always willig to help others however I can.

As a team leader

When leading team I tend to lean towards keeping everyone on track and in good mood and try to do everything in my power to help them through hardships. I usually try to befriend every memeber in the team as I think it boosts the overall performance of the team.

Individually

When working alone I can easily work for 10+ hours straight with minor 5-10 minute breaks as I get immersed into the process. I get in to flow very easily whenever I work by myself, also my quality of work seems to be bit more consistent as I have same working conditions all the time.
